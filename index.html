    Ecommerce webite

    For our fast food e-commerce website serving Mati City, our team leveraged a collaborative approach, harnessing the power of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL to craft a seamless and efficient platform. The heart of our site lies in its functionalities—specifically, the ability for users to effortlessly add items to their cart and for administrators to seamlessly introduce new products into our database To ensure a smooth user experience, we've implemented a responsive and visually appealing design using HTML and CSS. Our frontend development team meticulously crafted each page to be intuitive and engaging, enabling customers to navigate through our offerings with ease. JavaScript then steps in to provide dynamic functionality, allowing users to add products to their cart without having to refresh the page, thus enhancing the overall shopping experience. On the backend, PHP takes the reins, orchestrating the communication between the frontend and our MySQL database. When a user adds a product to their cart, PHP processes the request, validating the inputs, and securely updating the user's cart in our database. Similarly, when an administrator adds a new product, PHP ensures the integrity of the data, handling form submissions and storing the product details in our MySQL database.
